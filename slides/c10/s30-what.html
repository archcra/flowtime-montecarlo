The Monte Carlo Method

Monte Carlo不是一种固定的算法 
  There is no single Monte Carlo method – any attempt to define one will inevitably
  leave out valid examples –
  but many simulations follow this pattern:

  - model a system as a (series of) probability density functions (PDFs);
  - repeatedly sample from the PDFs;
  - tally/compute the statistics of interest.

  但它的模式是这样的：
  1. 将系统以一定的概率密度函数建模 (Probability Density Functions (PDFs))
  2. 不断从模型中取样: Sampling From Probability Density Functions (PDFs)
  3. 统计、计算
